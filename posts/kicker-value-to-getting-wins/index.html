Here, you can see the Cowboys have a strong kicker based on points scored, however, that kicker is responsible for almost 35% of his teams’ points. Even the best kicker in the world cannot make up for a lack of scoring by the rest of his team. The Lions are a display of how overall team strength is affected when both the kicker and the offense do their jobs in running up the score.
